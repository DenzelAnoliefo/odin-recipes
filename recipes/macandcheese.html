Mac & Cheese

Description

Prepare for the greatest comfort food to ever grace the planet! With this recipe, you'll enjoy a creamy, cheesy, delicious baked mac and cheese that is sure to be the next talking point at family dinner.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Bread Crumbs

Recipe

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
